Amazing Brisket Chili

This is an amazing brisket chili recipe that everybody
is going to be asking about.


Ingredients

  • Smoked Brisket
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Beef Broth
  • Tomato Paste
  • Jalapenos
  • Pablanos
  • Habaneros
  • Chili Beans
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Smoke Brisket As Required
  2. Cut up all peppers and onions
  3. Heat Oil and cook peppers and onions for 5 minutes
  4. Add seasoning and cook 5 more minutes
  5. Add Tomatoe Sauce, Beans and Broth and cook 1 hour
  6. Add more chili Powder
  7. Add meat and cook another hour
