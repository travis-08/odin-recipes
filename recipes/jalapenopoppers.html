Jalpeno Poppers

Awesome Jalapeno Poppers

This is an amazing brisket chili recipe that everybody
is going to be asking about.


Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Little Smokies
  • Cream Cheese
  • Brown Sugar
  • Toothpicks

Steps

  1. Cut Jalapenos in half lengthwise and remove seeds
  2. Put smokie in Jalpeno and wrap with bacon, hold with toothpick
  3. Sprinkle Brown Sugar on everything
  4. Put in smoker for 2-4 hours
