White Chili

Amazing White Chili

This is an amazing white chili recipe that everybody
is going to be asking about.


Ingredients

  • Cooked Chicken Breast, Pulled into large shreds
  • Chicken Broth
  • Habaneros
  • Chili Beans
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Sour Cream
  • shredded cheddar
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Add oil to pan
  2. Cook Peppers and onions 5 minutes
  3. Add seasoning and cook 5 more minutes
  4. Add broth and beans and cook 1 hour
  5. Add chicken and more chili powder, cook 1 more hour
  6. Add Cilantro
  7. Serve, Add sour cream and shredded cheddar to servings
